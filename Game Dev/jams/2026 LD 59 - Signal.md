## Theme: "Signal"
- in what sense
	- warning/awareness
		- flare, flashing lights, traffic lights, beacon, light house
	- demonstration
		- whistle, gesture, signboard, turn signal, hand signal, semaphore
	- like signal-to-noise
		- radio chatter, cross-talk, chirp, electrical, wifi, reception, signal processing, sine/square/triangle/sawtooth, circuits, aliens, metal detector, interlacing

- engineer in postapocalyptic wasteland harvests electronics from garbage heaps to create devices to navigate the world (alt: Geordi from Star Trek)
	- start poor with a stick to hit bug enemies
	- metal detector for currency
	- other detector for new exits/entrances
	- ray gun for killing basic enemies
	- other detector for seeing invisible enemies
		- later introduce rooms which are totally dark but adequate use of the tool can allow both navigation of the room and the enemies
